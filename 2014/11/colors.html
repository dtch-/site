Colorschemes... What are they?

2014, Nov. 18

A colorscheme is something that, quite obviously, sets the scheme of colors of a utility. Whether it's vim, weechat, or even your terminal, most programs allow for custom colors.

This page shall be marked as a 'living' page, meaning that I will be returning to update it as I add more colors to my collection.

  • terminal: z-brah - z3bra.org
